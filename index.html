Gianfranco Di Pietro

I'm a father, teacher, civil and environmental engineer, web and mobile developer, researcher... and this is my personal website. You are Welcome

 

Teaching

I am a personal and professionals trainer/teacher on these topics:

  • Geographical Information Systems
  • Topography and Geodesy
  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Building design of structures

In last decade I've worked as Topography teacher in North-East Italy and Sicily. I've collaborate with professionals and enterprises in vocational education. Currently I work as support teacher for student with disability and special needs in Sicily.

Educational projects with my contribute:


  • The Green School Network: a CLIL project to spread environmental sustainability over High Schools.
  • Geofunction Academy: an italian blog with many tutorial and tips on geospatial topics.
  • ICF: International Classification of Functionalities v.2002 mind map schema. Last update 2016
